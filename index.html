History

Instagram was started in San Francisco by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger ,who initially tried creating a platform similar to Foursquare but then turned their attention exclusively to phto sharing.

The initial release of instagram was on October 6,2010 exclusively on IOS.

A version for Android devices was released a year and half later, in April 2012, followed by a feature-limited website interface in November 2012, and apps for Windows 10 Mobile and Windows 10 in April 2016 and October 2016 respectively.
